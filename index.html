Troubleshooting Mixed Notes

"Tasting Bitterness and Sourness at once?" Show Fix

• Uneven Extraction: Water is channeling through specific parts of the coffee bed.

• Technique Fix: Use a WDT tool to declump. Ensure a perfectly level tamp using a flat tamper (avoid curved).

• The Grind: If you grind too fine, it creates extreme resistance leading to puck cracks and "spray" channeling.
